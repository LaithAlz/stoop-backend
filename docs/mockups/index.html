Each mockup is the same product moment — Maria's 2 AM no-heat text, triaged and drafted, waiting for your approval — told in three completely different visual languages. All are live HTML with animations: open them, hover around, hit the approve buttons.

THE APP DESIGN ★ FOUNDER-APPROVED 2026-07-05 · CLARITY

Clarity — brass plaque & margin notes

The current app design authority. Enamel severity plaques that read as plain words (Emergency / Can't wait / Whenever), Stoop's reasoning as always-visible margin notes, a perforated undo ticket, three system type voices, no paper texture. The dashboard rebuild implements this screen by screen.

 → PREVIOUS APP AUTHORITY · HERITAGE LIGHT

Heritage — The app (simple)

Three tabs: Home, Places, Settings. Stoop speaks first-person plain English ("Here's what I'd like to say — just tap OK"), all machinery hidden behind "why?" disclosures, big buttons, working send-with-undo, and the emergency takeover (demo button, top right). Designed for the least technical landlord in Ontario — in the warm Heritage light style the live site uses (emergency screen stays dark, per the design system).

Brownstone — Landing page

The public-facing landing page in the blended style, with an interactive triage demo in the hero — pick "no heat", "ceiling leak", or "parking question" and watch Stoop classify, reason, and draft live. Plus how-it-works, severity contract, trust ladder, and pricing.

 → DIRECTION 04 · THE BLEND ★

Brownstone

Night Desk's dark console + Front Porch's warm bento and springy physics, minus the cartoon. Warm charcoal, amber and terracotta; pill nav, reasoning trace, ticker, night-grid calendar, and a rotated SENT stamp borrowed from the Times.

 → DIRECTION 01

Night Desk

A premium dispatch console. Dark phosphor-amber terminal energy — live ticker, agent reasoning trace, pulsing severity dots, a "SENT" stamp on approve. Sells the "it works while you sleep" promise hard.

 → DIRECTION 02

The Stoop Times

Editorial brutalism — your night, printed as a morning newspaper. Giant masthead, red marquee, telegram-style tenant messages, rubber-stamp "APPROVED" animation. Unforgettable and very shareable.

 → DIRECTION 03

Front Porch

Warm neighbourhood bento — terracotta, butter and dusk blue with springy hover physics, sticker severity chips, a candy-stripe trust meter, and confetti when you approve. Friendly where the others are intense.
