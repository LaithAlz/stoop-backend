# Channel Playbook — the marketing operating system

> Written 2026-06-12. This is the *operating layer*: every channel, its
> status, weekly actions, time budget, and kill/scale criteria — one page
> to run marketing from. Scripts and specs live in their own docs (linked);
> this doc says **what to do this week and how to judge it**.

## Operating rules

1. **One experimental channel at a time.** Active channels run on
   routine; new channels get a 4-week test with a pre-written kill
   criterion. Never three half-tested channels.
2. **Every contact gets a source tag** (waitlist `source` / the tracking
   sheet). A channel without attribution is a hobby.
3. Time-boxed: marketing is ~8 hrs/week during the build (it's the
   founder's second job until pilots), structured below.
4. Judge channels on **qualified conversations and activations**, not
   impressions. A Reddit post that brings 3 pilots beats one that brings
   500 visitors.

## Local search plan (the missing channel)

Stoop is a service-area software business — local search won't be the #1
channel, but "Toronto/Ontario + landlord" queries are qualified and
cheap, and **reviews are the trust signal** every other channel benefits
from.

**Setup (1 hour, the week the domain is live):**
1. **Google Business Profile** — service-area business (no address
   shown), category "Software company" + secondary "Property management
   company"; service area: Ontario (or GTA to start). Name exactly
   "Stoop" (no keyword stuffing — suspension risk). Link the site, add
   the wordmark + 3 product screenshots, write the 750-char description
   in plain-English brand voice.
2. **Bing Places + Apple Business Connect** — 20 minutes total, set and
   forget.
3. **NAP consistency:** the entity name, domain, and (Twilio main)
   number identical everywhere; listed once in the footer of the site.
4. **Directory citations that matter for this niche only:** SOLO member
   directory, Ontario REI groups' vendor lists, Clutch/G2 later (G2
   matters for the PM segment, not landlords). Skip the 50-citation spam
   services entirely.

**Reviews engine (the real point):**
- Ask every happy pilot for a **Google review** at the same two moments
  as the referral ask (after a save; after a glowing check-in) — one ask
  per landlord, ever. Target: 10 reviews by end of pilots.
- Review reply rule: founder replies to every review within 48 h, plain
  voice, no corporate-speak.
- Reviews feed everything: GBP ranking, the landing page ("rated ★ by
  Ontario landlords" once ≥5), and FB-group credibility when someone
  searches the name mid-thread — **assume every prospect googles
  "Stoop reviews" before paying.**

**Local content (folds into SEO cadence, not extra work):** the city
dimension on existing topics — "Toronto's 21°C heat rule", "no heat
Ottawa: landlord duties" — one city-variant post/month from H1; full
programmatic city pages only when content shows traction.

**KPI:** GBP views → website clicks (free in GBP dashboard), review
count/rating. Kill criterion: none — at 15 min/week maintenance this
channel is never worth killing, just never worth more time.

## The weekly rhythm (build phase, ~8 h)

| Day | Block | What |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | 2 h | Content: write/edit the SEO post or tool copy of the fortnight |
| Tue | 1.5 h | Kijiji/FRBO: 10 personalized DMs + log them |
| Wed | 1 h | Communities: genuinely answer 3–5 threads (Reddit/FB); zero pitching until week 4 |
| Thu | 1 h | Nurture + pipeline: Touch-0/1 emails, reply to everything, update the tracking sheet |
| Fri | 30 m | Metrics: source-tagged signups by channel, reply rates; decide next week's single experiment |
| float | 2 h | Meetup when scheduled / video session / GBP setup week |

## Scale/kill criteria per test

- A channel test = 4 weeks at the stated effort. **Scale** if it produced
  ≥3 qualified conversations or ≥10 source-tagged signups. **Kill** (or
  park with a re-entry trigger) otherwise — write the number down before
  starting, not after.
- Standing exception: SEO and reviews are compounding channels — they're
  judged at 6 months, not 4 weeks.
